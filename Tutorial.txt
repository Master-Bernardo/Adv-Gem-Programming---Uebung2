2 player fight against each other in a wizards duel

the first player with 3 wins wins the game, hit t to restart

Movement:

player1:
w - jump
a - move left
d - move right
q and e - rotate your aim
f - fire your magic projectile 

player2:
arrow up - jump
arrow left - move left
arrow right - move right
i and p - rotate your aim
o - fire your magic projectile
